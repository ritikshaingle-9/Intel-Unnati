Government Services

Aadhar Card

Find the nearest government offices for Aadhar/Land registration offices. Ensure you have your documents ready and a filled application form.

Passport

Apply for your passport online or at the nearest passport office. Don't forget to bring your proof of identity and address.

Ration Card

Get your ration card issued by the government. You can apply online or at your local food office with required documents.

Voter ID

Register for your voter ID card at the nearest election office. You will need proof of age and residence.

Driving License

Apply for a driving license through the local transport office. Make sure you have a learner's permit and necessary documents.
